Text to DNA Sequence Converter

DNA is comprised of four nucleotides, A,C, G, and T.

What this website does is convert a text file of your choice to a DNA sequence, then compare it to known virus DNA sequences.
Depending on how great the similarities are, it will tell you if your text document has a "virus" or not.
